Ode To Cookies and Popcorn

In life, there are not many things more comforting than cookies and popcorn. I wanted to make a little website to show my appreciation for these things.

Ode to popcorn

Oh, you who pop so well my friend,

the only way for me to calm

my mind that races through the heat

I get home to smell your sweet aroma

of comfort and salt with a hint of warmth
